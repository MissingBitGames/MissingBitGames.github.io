Rollox

made in New Zealand

The Story of Rollox

The inspiration for Rollox came when our baby was born at the hospital in Lower Hutt. I took her big sister for a walk and we found the Labyrinth.

Unlike a maze, it's a single path without branches.

The original working title of the game was Labyrinth.I've developed it over the last two years.

Easy to pickup but a challenge to master

Rollox will be released for Android in Q4 of 2019 or early 2020.

GamePlay

You can clear any chain of two or more tiles that are touching.
Diagonals don't count. Each level introduces a new path

You win by clearing all the chains of tiles

You fail if there are tiles left and no more possible moves

You get more points for each tile in a chain based on the chain’s length. Each time you clear a tile you get a chance of earning coins. You can get up to 500 coins per tile cleared.

Episodes

Introduction

A collection of levels designed to ease you into the challenges of Rollox. They start off with a small array of tightly wound tracks. These grow in difficulty into large rows with lots of spaces.

Steampunk

These will challenge you further, introducing two special tiles.

Blocks can't be popped and have to reach the end of the track.

Bombs when exploded chain with all connecting types incluing blocks.

You can unlock episode two with coins earned in game OR by using IAP(In app purchase).

Fluffy

These cute creatures will be the bane of your existence. Introducing two more special tile types.

Bubbles need to be popped before the title can be cleared.

Multi-tiles can form chains with both tile types.

This episode is IAP only

Challange Levels

There are also extra challange levels which offer a unique spin on the game. They are unlocked with each episode. They can be played by paying coins.
