The Story of Rollox

I came up with the idea for rollox when my second baby was being born at the hospital in Lower Hutt. I took the older girl for a walk and we found the Labrynth. This was the inspration for the gameplay

You win th game by clearing all the gems. You can clear any two gems that are adjacent, but not diagonal. Each level introduces a new path

It's easy to play but challanging to master
